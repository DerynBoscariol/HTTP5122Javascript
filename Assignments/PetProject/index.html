Margarita!

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 1/2 oz triple sec
  • 1 oz lime juice

Using a jigger, measure 2 oz of tequila, 1/2 oz of triple sec, and 1 oz of lime juice into a shaker with ice cubes and shake until chilled. Pour into a glass over fresh ice. Optional garnishes: salt rim, lime wedge.

Tequila Sunrise!

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 4 oz orange juice
  • 1/4 oz grenadine

Using a jigger, measure 2 oz of tequila and 4 oz of orange juice into a highball glass with ice and stir. Top off with 1/4 oz of grenadine and let sink to the bottom of the glass creating the sunrise effect. Optional garnishes: cherry, orange wedge.

Long Island Iced Tea!

  • 3/4 oz vodka
  • 3/4 oz rum
  • 3/4 oz tequila
  • 3/4 oz gin
  • 3/4 oz triple sec
  • 3/4 oz lemon juice
  • Cola, to top

Using a jigger, measure 3/4 oz vodka, 3/4 oz rum, 3/4 oz tequila, 3/4 oz gin, 3/4 oz triple sec, and 3/4 oz lemon juice into a shaker with ice and shake until chilled. Pour into a highball glass with fresh ice and top with cola. Optional garnish: lemon wedge.
